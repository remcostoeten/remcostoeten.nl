# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: 2026-04-05*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** Personal portfolio and technical blog of Remco Stoeten, a Dutch Frontend Engineer.
**What it does:** Showcases professional work experience, featured projects, and technical insights through an MDX-driven blog. Integrates real-time GitHub and Spotify data to reflect active development and personal interests.
**Product category:** Personal Portfolio / Engineering Blog
**Product type:** Performance-optimized Next.js Web Application
**Business model:** Personal Branding / Professional Showcase

## Target Audience
**Target companies:** Tech-forward companies, SaaS startups, E-commerce platforms, and Engineering teams looking for experienced Frontend talent.
**Decision-makers:** Engineering Managers, CTOs, Tech Leads, and Technical Recruiters.
**Primary use case:** Demonstrating technical expertise, design sensibility (Graphic Design background), and professional history to potential employers or collaborators.
**Jobs to be done:**
- Validate Remco's technical skills and experience level.
- Review past project work and contributions.
- Read technical articles to gauge depth of knowledge.
- Get a sense of Remco's personality and work ethic (through "Activity" and "About" sections).

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** Recruiters and hiring managers often struggle to see the "person behind the CV" or verify actual coding style and architectural thinking.
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- Static CVs don't show real-time activity or "alive" code.
- Generic LinkedIn profiles lack personality and deep technical showcases.
**What it costs them:** Time spent in early-round technical screenings that could have been avoided with a comprehensive portfolio.
**Emotional tension:** Uncertainty when hiring for senior/lead roles where both design-eye and technical-rigor are required.

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- Dual-background: Degree in *Graphic Design* combined with *8 years* of engineering experience.
- Real-time transparency: Integrated GitHub/Spotify activity shows he is an active, passionate developer.
- High Performance: Built with Next.js 16/React 19, Tailwind v4, and optimized for speed (Lighthouse audited).
**How we do it differently:** Blending premium aesthetics with transparent, raw data (activity feeds) and high-quality technical writing.
**Why that's better:** It builds immediate trust and showcases a rare intersection of design-led thinking and engineering excellence.
